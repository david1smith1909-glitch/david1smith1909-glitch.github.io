QA Metrics for Executives

Author: Alok Kumar, Enterprise QA Advisor

Metrics Leadership Actually Uses

Executives care about release reliability and business stability—not raw test counts.

Essential Metrics

  • Defect Leakage Rate
  • Change Failure Rate
  • Deployment Frequency
  • Mean Time to Detect (MTTD)
  • Mean Time to Recover (MTTR)

Dashboard Principles

Use trend-based dashboards that answer one question clearly: is release risk improving or worsening over time?
