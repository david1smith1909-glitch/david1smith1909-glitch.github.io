Release Readiness Checklist

Author: Alok Kumar, Enterprise QA Advisor

Why Release Readiness Matters

High-growth SaaS teams often move fast, but speed without readiness creates instability. A release readiness checklist ensures engineering, QA, product, and operations align before deployment.

Core Checklist Areas

  • Feature completion against acceptance criteria
  • Regression and automation status
  • Critical integrations validated
  • Security and compliance checks
  • Rollback and recovery strategy confirmed
  • Monitoring and alerting configured

Go / No-Go Decision

Final release approval should be based on risk visibility, not deadlines. QA provides risk assessment while leadership makes informed release decisions.
